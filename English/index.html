Features of the region

Lena river in Russia, the largest river of Middle Siberia, the largest of the Russian rivers, whose pool entirely lies within the country. Also it is the largest river in the world which is completely flowing in the permafrost region. She has a lot of the inflows interesting from the point of view of fishing. For example, on the 606th kilometer Muna falls into her. Muna's pool won't be affected by almost industrial activity and it is seldom visited by people. The surrounding nature has remained in original state, is very picturesque. The river is pure, fish: whitefish, lenok, taimen, pike, perch Yelets and others. Here freely many animals and birds feel. And also is the mass of other rivers and rivulets where we make throwing on a seaplane.
